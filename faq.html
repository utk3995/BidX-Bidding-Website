Cancellations

When are returns not possible?

There are a few cases where we cannot support returns. They are:
When product is damaged because of use or when product is not in the same condition as you received it
When any consumable item has been used or installed
When anything is missing from the package you've received including price tags, labels, original packing, freebies & accessories
When non-returnable items are involved such as innerwear. lingerie etc.
When items are tampered with or have missing serial numbers


What should I do if I want to exchange a product and it is either sold out or not available?

You can choose to return the product for a full refund from My Orders.
Please note that only certain items can be returned or exchanged. For more information, check the seller return policy on the product page.

Payment

What is Cash on Delivery?

With Cash on Delivery (CoD), you can pay for your order in cash when it is actually being delivered.
You can also pay with your Credit or Debit card if the courier person is carrying a card machine.

How can I pay for an order?

You can choose to pay for an order with any of the below methods:

  • Cash on Delivery
  • Net Banking
  • Visa, MasterCard, Maestro and American Express Credit or Debit cards issued in India and 21 other countries
  • Gift Card
  • Casanda Wallet - For products listed by seller WS Retail

Shopping

Why do I see different prices for the same product?

You could see different prices for the same product, as it could be listed by many Sellers.

What does 'Permanently Discontinued' or 'Out of Print' mean?

This product is no longer available because it is obsolete and/or its production has been discontinued.

Can I order a product that is Out of Stock or Temporarily Unavailable?

Unfortunately, products listed as Out of Stock or Temporarily Unavailable are not available for sale. Please use the 'Notify Me' feature to be informed of the
product's availability with Sellers on Casanda.

What is My Wallet in my Casanda account?

Your Wallet is a digital payment mode which can be used for making quick and hassle-free payments on Casanda.
It has the following sections for ease of payment:

  1. Casanda Money
  2. Gift Cards
  3. WSR Credit
  4. Saved Cards
*Casanda Money is powered by FX Mart. T&C apply.

Why should I use Casanda Wallet?

Casanda Wallet is the fastest and easiest way to pay for all your purchases since you don’t have to remember your card or bank details when you shop with us.
Besides offering you a choice between multiple payment modes at one place, it is a secure and hassle-free way to shop for your favourite products on Casanda!
